About me

I received my B.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees in information and communication engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China, in 2018 and 2023, respectively.
I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University College Dublin, Dublin, Ireland (November 2023 - October 2024), and a Postdoctoral Research Assistant at the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Queen Mary University of London, London, U.K. (April 2024 - November 2024). I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science, Queen Mary University of London, London, U.K.. I focus on using mathematical tools to analyze and optimize the performance of wireless transmission.
